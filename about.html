About Nockitout

What we do: Nockitout is software that ingests your inbound leads from forms, calls, ads, and email—scores them with AI, and alerts your team to the highest-quality opportunities with simple daily updates.

Our Mission

Help teams respond faster and sell smarter by turning messy inbound activity into a clear, ranked list of who to call next.

Signal over noiseFilter spam and low-intent leads; surface the ones that convert.
Speed to leadPush timely notifications to inbox and SMS so the right rep acts first.
Own your stackWorks with the tools you already use—Sheets, CRMs, email, and phone.

How it works

  1. Connect sources. Forms, call transcripts/voicemail, ad leads, email—point them to Nockitout.
  2. Score & classify. AI ranks intent, fit, urgency, and flags spam/duplicates automatically.
  3. Notify & track. Daily digest and instant pings highlight who to contact first with reasoning attached.
